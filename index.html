conclusion

The Constitution establishes a parliamentary form of government with a President as the constitutional head, a Council of Ministers led by the Prime Minister, and a two-house parliament (Rajya Sabha and Lok Sabha).

Sidebar

Note:he Constitution can be amended, but the amendment process is designed to be more difficult than ordinary legislation, ensuring stability. The Supreme Court and the High Courts are independent bodies, responsible for interpreting the Constitution and upholding the rule of law.
